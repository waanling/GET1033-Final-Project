Final Project


This is my GET1033 Final Project, a responsive online portfolio that is customizable based on my personal preferences. It can be a portfolio for potential employers or even friends, as they can easily browse through online to know more about me. This project is designed on Adobe XD and coded from scratch on Brackets, before being transferred to sublime text for convenience.
